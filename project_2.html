Curating a Children’s Book Experience

Background

My client is a writer and a grandmother. She had a story that she wrote about her grandchildren that she wanted to make into a children’s book.

Problem

The goal was to design a website and create social media marketing for my client’s children’s book.

Process

In my capacity as a designer, I played a pivotal role in developing a sophisticated website dedicated to the book. This entailed crafting a visually captivating layout that harmonized with the book's monochromatic color schemes and characters. I ensured seamless navigation for both parents and young readers. Additionally, I orchestrated a strategic social media marketing campaign aimed at generating excitement and directing traffic to the website.

Results

Social media marketing facilitated direct sales through an integrated e-commerce platform, resulting in a measurable increase in book purchases.
